Admission

The admission process begins with registration.

REGISTRATION:

Parents who want admission should register for admission Test. Registration form will be issued to all the new students on payment of form fee.
PIA Employees will procedure medical card of their children whereas non-PIA will produce birth certificate of their children for issuing of registration form.

For Montessori Interview of child and Parents
For Prep-I to class IV English, Urdu, Math’s
For Other Classes English, Urdu, Math’s, Science

Result of Admission Test will be finalized with a week. The students who will qualify the admission test will be issued Admission form for their respected classes. Copy of birth certificate is essentially required for classes. Prep & I. Transfer certificate will be required fir other classes. The principal, PIAMSS is the final authority for admission. The account section will receive the necessary dues from the student’s parents and will issue Admission Slip, a copy of which will be sent to the concerned class teacher/Vice Principal of the section for further necessary action. The school Superintendent will also allot GR no to all the new admitted students as per record provided by the parents.


All the New students must submit attested copy of the Birth Certificate, TC having passed the previous class of studying in the same class for which the admission is being sought. Care should be taken to check that these documents are genuine and pertain to the same student by the school superintendent. All students who have been admitted should be given the complete package of admission, which are as follow:

  1. Addresses of Uniform and Books and Stationary suppliers
  2. Code of Conduct of students
  3. Syllabus
