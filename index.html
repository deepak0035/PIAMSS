Our Philosophy

We facilitate a secure and cultured atmosphere for our pupils to feel safe and learn to live uprightly. We believe that every child is unique and that children learn better when they are in a happy state of mind. Our friendly and positive environment spreads space for each student to grow as a cheerful and independent learner.

Library

A library also has a disciplinary function. It helps in developing a sense of discipline among the students as they are required to sit in quietude while carrying out their study or research.

Admission

We aim to fulfil child’s educational and development needs in a nurturing environment. Our team is happy to support you and answer all your queries related to the admission process.

Lab

Children are able to think deeply about the theories and concepts they were taught in class through their practical application. It’s like the entire theory just comes to life and holds new meaning to them. Lab experiments also improve a child’s reasoning abilities.

Experienced teachers.

We have experienced and well qualified teachers who teach in a disciplinary manner.

Sports

Our school also focuses on the sport activities for children. Sports activities develop physical fitness, self-esteem and confidence. Children improve movement and coordination skills and learn to listen and follow instructions and basic tactics. They learn to lead, follow and be part of a team and also learn about fair play.

PIA Model Secondary School

The PIA Model Secondary School was set up in 1980 to provide high quality education to the children of PIA’s employees at an affordable fee. Situated at a distance from the hubbub of the city, the school is housed in a modern building that spans over 10 acres (40,000 m2) of land and is staffed by teachers dedicated to empowering children with knowledge and skills for the future. Equal emphasis is placed on curricular and extra curricular activities in order to produce well-rounded, holistic, and responsible citizens.

  • Experienced & Qualified Faculty
  • Neat & Clean Environment
  • Computer Labs Equipped with Internet Facility
  • Science Labs / Library
  • Extra Curricular Activities
  • Picnic & Study tour
  • Vast Play Grounds & Sports Activities
  • Best Results (BSE), Karachi
  • Safe & Secure Location
